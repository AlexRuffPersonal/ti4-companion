# Game Phase Icons

Destination: `ti4-companion-web/public/icons/phases/`

Phase icons keep their colours — each phase has a distinct colour association in the app's UI. These appear in the GameHeader to indicate the current phase. Transparent background on all.

---

### strategy-phase.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon for the Strategy Phase in Twilight Imperium 4. In this phase each player drafts one of 8 numbered Strategy Cards that determine turn order and grant unique abilities for the round. Icon concept: a row of three rectangles representing face-down cards, with the centre card raised above the others as if being picked or selected. Suggests deliberate choice. Fill: #58a6ff (blue). Transparent background. 64x64px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text.

---

### action-phase.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon for the Action Phase in Twilight Imperium 4. This is the main phase where players take turns activating systems, moving fleets, and playing cards — dynamic and urgent, the longest and most complex phase. Icon concept: a bold angular lightning bolt, tall and sharp, suggesting energy, speed, and decisive action. Fill: #e3b341 (amber). Transparent background. 64x64px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text.

---

### status-phase.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon for the Status Phase in Twilight Imperium 4. In this phase players score objectives, ready exhausted cards, repair damaged units, and return command tokens to their pools — a moment of consolidation and reset. Icon concept: two curved arrows forming a full circle (a circular refresh or reload symbol), suggesting cycling, readying, and reset. Fill: #3fb950 (green). Transparent background. 64x64px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text.

---

### agenda-phase.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon for the Agenda Phase in Twilight Imperium 4. In this phase two Agenda cards are revealed and all players vote using their planets' Influence values, then the winning law or directive takes effect and alters the game. Icon concept: a gavel silhouette — a rectangular mallet head on a handle held at a slight angle as if mid-strike, suggesting law, decision, and binding resolution. Fill: #d4a017 (gold). Transparent background. 64x64px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text.
