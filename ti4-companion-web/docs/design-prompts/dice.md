# Dice Icons

Destination: `ti4-companion-web/public/icons/dice/`

The combat die is neutral — it appears in dice result panels that are already coloured by hit/miss state, so the die itself should be a clean steel colour. Transparent background.

---

### d10.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon of a ten-sided die (d10). In Twilight Imperium 4 all combat rolls use d10s — players roll at or below a unit's combat value to score a hit. The d10 has a characteristic elongated diamond-like outline: a tall shape with a pointed top and pointed bottom, with five upper faces and five lower faces implied by angled edge lines running from a central equatorial ridge. One prominent face shows two small circular dots (pips) rather than numerals, suggesting the number ten. The die must be clearly identifiable as a ten-sided die and not a d6 or d8. Fill: #c9d1d9 (pale steel) for the die body, with inner face edge lines in a slightly darker tone. Transparent background. 64x64px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients.
