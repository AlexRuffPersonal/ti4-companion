# Unit Icons

Destination: `ti4-companion-web/public/icons/units/`

Ships and ground units use **white fills** (`#ffffff`) so they can be CSS colour-tinted at runtime to match each faction's colour. No faction markings. Transparent background on all.

---

### carrier.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon of a Twilight Imperium 4 Carrier spaceship. Side-view silhouette. The Carrier is a wide, boxy rectangular transport — a flying cargo barge: broad flat hull with shallow depth, blunt nose, two small stubby down-angled stabiliser fins at the rear, and two short engine pods at the back. No swept wings. Workmanlike and blocky, the opposite of sleek. White fill (#ffffff) on transparent background. 64x64px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text, no faction markings.

### cruiser.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon of a Twilight Imperium 4 Cruiser spaceship. Side-view silhouette. The Cruiser is a mid-sized warship with an elongated hull that tapers to a sharp point at the bow, gently swept-back wings angling downward from the mid-body, and a single engine cluster at the stern. Elegant and fast-looking — clearly larger than a fighter, clearly smaller than a dreadnought, with a graceful swept form. White fill (#ffffff) on transparent background. 64x64px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text, no faction markings.

### fighter.svg

Compact delta-wing or arrowhead shape: broad triangular wings relative to a very short body, small cockpit bump at the centre, two tiny engine exhausts. Must look clearly much smaller and simpler than every other ship in the set. White fill (#ffffff) on transparent background. 64x64px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text, no faction markings.
