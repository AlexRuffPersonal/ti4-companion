# Technology Category Icons

Destination: `ti4-companion-web/public/icons/tech/`

Technology icons keep their game colours — the four tech categories are colour-coded in TI4 (green, yellow, blue, red) and players identify them by colour as much as shape. Transparent background on all.

---

### biotic.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon for the Biotic technology category in Twilight Imperium 4 (the green tech tree). Abstract symbol combining organic and scientific motifs: a stylised DNA double helix rendered in angular geometric segments, or a leaf-circuit hybrid where organic curves intersect with straight circuit-board traces. Should feel alive and growing yet technological. Fill: #3fb950 (green). Transparent background. 64x64px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text.

---

### cybernetic.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon for the Cybernetic technology category in Twilight Imperium 4 (the yellow tech tree). Abstract symbol suggesting computing and enhancement: a stylised integrated circuit chip in a square housing with pin legs extending from the sides, or a human brain silhouette overlaid with circuit traces. Angular, precise, and geometric — suggesting machine logic. Fill: #e3b341 (amber). Transparent background. 64x64px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text.

---

### propulsion.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon for the Propulsion technology category in Twilight Imperium 4 (the blue tech tree). Abstract symbol suggesting faster-than-light travel and drive systems: a stylised engine nozzle or warp ring — a circular ring with a central exhaust cone and three or four speed-lines emanating outward, suggesting thrust and velocity. Fill: #58a6ff (blue). Transparent background. 64x64px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text.

---

### warfare.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon for the Warfare technology category in Twilight Imperium 4 (the red tech tree). Abstract symbol suggesting weapons and military force: a stylised targeting reticle — a circle bisected by crosshair lines with small range-tick marks — or an angular stylised cannon barrel seen from three-quarters view. Aggressive and direct. Fill: #f85149 (red). Transparent background. 64x64px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text.
