# Economy Icons

Destination: `ti4-companion-web/public/icons/economy/`

Economy icons keep their colours — gold for trade goods and victory points (both are prestige/currency items in TI4), neutral steel for commodities (raw faction resources). Transparent background on all.

---

### trade-good.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon for a Trade Good in Twilight Imperium 4. Trade Goods are gold hexagonal tokens on the physical board — small hexagonal coins used as universal currency between factions. Icon concept: a regular hexagon with flat top and bottom (not pointy-top), a thin inner border ring suggesting a coin edge, and a small bold star or gem shape centred in the face. Must read as a coin or token, not a planet tile hex. Fill: #d4a017 (gold). Transparent background. 64x64px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text.

---

### commodity.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon for a Commodity in Twilight Imperium 4. Commodities are a faction's raw trade resource — represented physically as coloured cubes or small tokens. Icon concept: a small cube viewed from an isometric three-quarter angle — three visible faces with clear edge lines distinguishing them, suggesting a solid physical resource block or crate. Must look like a resource cube, not a coin. Fill: #c9d1d9 (pale steel). Transparent background. 64x64px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text.

---

### victory-point.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon for a Victory Point in Twilight Imperium 4. Victory Points are the winning metric of the game, tracked on a central scoreboard. Icon concept: a clean five-pointed star with sharp angular facets and a well-proportioned body — not too thin or spindly, not too fat. Must look prestigious and important, the most visually prominent icon in the economy set. Fill: #d4a017 (gold). Transparent background. 64x64px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text.
