# Status Indicator Icons

Destination: `ti4-companion-web/public/icons/status/`

Status indicators keep their colours — green/amber/red/grey map directly to the game states they represent. These are small inline badges so they must be unambiguous at 16x16px; distinct shapes matter as much as colour. 32x32px canvas on all. Transparent background on all.

---

### ready.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon for the Ready or Active state in Twilight Imperium 4. A complete solid circle with a small upward-pointing arrow or pulse chevron centred inside it, suggesting full energy and readiness. The arrow must be clearly directional — pointing up, not inward. Fill: #3fb950 (green). Transparent background. 32x32px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text.

---

### exhausted.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon for the Exhausted state in Twilight Imperium 4. In the physical game, cards are rotated 90 degrees sideways when exhausted. Icon concept: a circle split into left and right halves where the right half is solid filled and the left half is only an outline — a half-moon split suggesting partial depletion. Must be clearly distinct from the Ready circle. Fill for solid half: #e3b341 (amber). Outline for empty half: #e3b341 (amber). Transparent background. 32x32px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text.

---

### damaged.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon for the Sustained Damage state in Twilight Imperium 4. Damaged units are rotated sideways to show they have absorbed one hit but are not destroyed. Icon concept: a shield silhouette with a bold diagonal crack splitting through it from upper-left to lower-right, suggesting structural damage absorbed but not yet destroyed. Fill: #f85149 (red). Transparent background. 32x32px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text.

---

### purged.svg

Flat minimalist SVG vector icon for the Purged state in Twilight Imperium 4 — permanently removed from the game, used for Heroes after activation and one-time-use cards. Icon concept: a circle outline (not filled, outline only) with a bold X drawn through its interior from corner to corner — suggesting something crossed out and gone forever. The circle should feel empty and broken. Stroke and X: #6e7681 (dim grey). Transparent background. 32x32px canvas. Flat vector, no gradients, no text.
